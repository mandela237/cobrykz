# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Local business owners — restaurants, clinics, salons, tradespeople, and similar service-based businesses — who know they need a better web presence but have been burned by overpriced agencies, slow timelines, or cookie-cutter results. They arrive skeptical and need to feel: "this person gets it and will actually deliver."

Secondary: ambitious founders and small companies who want a trusted digital partner for web apps, AI tooling, or automation — not just a website.

## Product Purpose

COBRYKZ is Mandela Atud's flagship lead-generation site. Its one job: convert skeptical visitors into booked discovery calls. The site must communicate quality through the experience itself — not through claims alone — so that a local business owner looks at it and thinks "if they built this, they can build mine."

Success = a filled contact form that leads to a real call.

## Brand Personality

Bold / Trustworthy / Modern

Voice is direct, confident, and founder-led — Mandela speaks in first person when needed, no anonymous agency voice. Warm enough to feel human, sharp enough to feel elite. No corporate distance.

## Anti-references

- **Big-agency corporate sites**: stock photos of handshakes, vague mission statements, cold and impersonal, nothing that feels like a real person is behind it
- **Generic SaaS/startup templates**: cream body backgrounds, hero-metric dashboards, identical card grids, eyebrows on every section — the saturated AI scaffold
- **Over-animated portfolio showpieces**: motion that competes with content, style that overshadows the founder's message

## Design Principles

1. **The site is the pitch** — every visual choice is a live proof of COBRYKZ's capability. If the site looks mediocre, the pitch fails before a word is read.
2. **Founder presence over agency anonymity** — Mandela's face, story, and voice are the differentiator. Design should frame him, not hide him behind polish.
3. **Earned confidence** — Bold claims must be immediately backed by proof: Lighthouse scores, real timelines, specific testimonials. Assertion without evidence reads as bluster.
4. **Warm premium, not cold luxury** — Approachable enough that a local business owner doesn't feel outpriced, premium enough that they feel they're hiring the best.
5. **No fluff, no filler** — Every section earns its place. If it doesn't move the visitor closer to booking a call, it shouldn't exist.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. High contrast on dark backgrounds (body text ≥ 4.5:1). Reduced motion respected via `prefers-reduced-motion`. Touch targets ≥ 44px on mobile. Keyboard navigable CTAs.
